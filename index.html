About Mood Garden

Mood Garden is a project aimed at improving mental health through plant therapy and technology. In today's fast-paced world, many people face increasing psychological pressure and emotional challenges. Mood Garden provides a unique solution by combining the natural healing power of plants with modern technology.

Our project is inspired by the growing awareness of mental health issues and the need for innovative solutions. By using plants as a medium, we aim to create a calming and therapeutic environment that helps users manage their emotions effectively.
